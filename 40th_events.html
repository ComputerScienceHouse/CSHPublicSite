40th Event Overview

Below you will find an overview of the events planned for 40th.  This is a general overview and subject to change.  More specifics will be announced as plans are finalized and the date of 40th approaches.


Friday, April 15th

Capture the Disk

Starting around dusk we'll be playing Capture the Disk.  Like the name suggests, Capture the Disk is a game similar to Capture the Flag, but with a CSH spin.  Rules will be explained prior to the start of the game. There will be an info session to teach new players and help old players learn the rule changes that have occurred over the years. The session will be at 7pm in NRH 1250.

Plates

After Capture the Disk is finished it's time to get plates! Small groups will organize and go to various restaurants.  Whether you like Mark's, Nick Tahou's, Steve T, or another place entirely this is the time to undo any kind of remotely healthy exercise Capture the Disk provided.

Messed Up Movie Night (FUMN)

Messed Up Movie Night (FUMN) is a gathering of like minded individuals looking for some good, ol' fashioned depraved, disturbing, or sometimes just plain awful, film. A few times a year The Committee descends upon house to screen approximately 10 hours of both feature and short films in one sitting. Some attendees have a skewed view of what the words "Messed Up" means... Messed Up is somewhat subjective... This subjectivity is best illustrated by the diversity of the list below as well as the reactions of the attendees themselves... Often arguments will break out after a particular film is screened between those who thought the film was Messed Up and those who think The Committee has lost it's mind.

Saturday, April 16th

Tours of Gene Polisseni Center

Saturday afternoon you can tour the new RIT hockey arena.  Tours for this event will be in smaller groups.  More information to come.

Tour Floor

Haven't been to floor in years? Want to come hang out? Tours of floor and probably many stories from past and present generations will occur all afternoon.

Formal Banquet

In the evening there will be a formal banquet held in the Davis Room of the Student Alumni Union at 7:00 PM Saturday. There will be a buffet of Italian food including Chicken Piccata, Tortellini, Salads, and a dessert of Tiramisu! There will be plenty of refreshments, a dance floor, and a brief speech on the current state of Computer Science House.

Sunday, April 17th

Goodbye Brunch

Brunch and one last get together before everyone heads out.  T-Shirts designed specially for 40th will be given out at this event.
